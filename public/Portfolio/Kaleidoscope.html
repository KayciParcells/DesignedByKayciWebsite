Kaleidoscope

Motion Graphics

This animation was created as an exploration of how a collection can be reimagined in a new way to be entertaining and engaging. Digitally recreating Verneuil’s 1926 art deco prints allowed for the colorful patterns to be put in motion to music in fun and creative ways. This further allowed for the development of an upbeat trailer that can catch one’s attention and explain the upcoming events of an exhibition at the New York Public Library.
