Columbia Advertising

Banner, Posters, Instagram Ads

By utilizing the photography of Columbia’s winter products and compositing them with snowy scenes, the viewer can see that Columbia products are what you want when the weather gets cold. Meanwhile, clean typography with a clear hierarchy informs customers of deals and products and gets them interested in the winter collection. Finally, simple Instagram posts were created to get customers excited about winter and thinking about buying Columbia products during the upcoming winter season.
