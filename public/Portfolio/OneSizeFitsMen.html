Updating Registration Page

UI Design | User Research | User Flows

The Request

This poster was developed as an advertisement for a community calisthenics group in New York City where individuals can get stronger in a positive, outdoor environment. The design was inspired by Sam Graham-Felsen’s Letter of Recommendation in the New York Times about how doing pull-ups at community parks allowed him to find an environment that encouraged growth and led him from not being able to do one pull up to being able to do many. Green and purple were chosen as the dominant colors in order to represent growth and power while showing that community calisthenics isn’t your typical gym community.

Primary Goal: Add ability for user to allow overspending on their Purchase Order and make pop-ups easier to use

Constraints:

  • Must remain pop-up (not become a webpage)
  • updates need to be small/easy for developers

Tools: Figma, Hotjar, pencil & paper

Platform: Desktop browser
