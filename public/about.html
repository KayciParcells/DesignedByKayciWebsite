About Me

I am creative, curious, and always seeking to learn something new. I have a background in software development, but prefer to blend my analytical and creative sides together through UX Design. Additionally, I have a vast design skillset that spans illustration, graphic design, UI Design, and motion graphics. If there is a skill I don't have in the software/design space I am open to learning it and due to my diverse background, can usually pick up new skills pretty quickly.

Outside of work, my biggest interest is defintitely bowling. It's another area where my keen attention to detail is challenged and my mind gets a distraction from everything else that happens in the day. I have grown to love the challenge of the game and as with everything in life, I am constantly looking to improve.


The Website

In case you were curious, for the creation of this website I chose to use Firebase for hosting and git for source control. Additionally, I kept the tech simple with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap for basic layout and styling along with a bit of vanilla javascript. While adding in my recent works, I did take a more modern approach with image formats by using the WebP format to improve performance and am so far very happy with that change. I do sometimes use this site as a bit of a sandbox to try out new things, so if you do ever spot something a bit out of the ordinary it may be me trying something new/testing an idea!
